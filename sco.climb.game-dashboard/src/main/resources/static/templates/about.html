About


In the digital age of technology, children are becoming less mobile and active in their daily lives. Our project aims to use technology to encourage, rather than hinder, healthy habits in elementary-aged students. Through close collaboration with current elementary teachers, students, and industry experts, our team has developed PROJECT SMART, an interactive, visual, and engaging web application that supports the classroom curriculum. We hope that PROJECT SMART will be delightful to both students and teachers, and we look forward to hearing your feedback!
